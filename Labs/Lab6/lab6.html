Lab 6: Favicon

Afsana Haque

  • What exactly is a favicon?
    • A favicon is the icon which correltaes with the website. It is located on the browser tab to the far left of the titles webpage.

  • How are favicons created?
    • Favicons are created by using different websites for examplemcode.org. These websites are used to create a logo which connects with your website/webpage.

  • How did I create the favicon for this site?
    • I created my favicon using code.org. First we created a paper sketch of how our favicon would look like. Then we practiced by creating a black and white version of our favicon on code.org. Finally, using the knowledge when doing the Black and White version of the favicon, we created a Colored version of our favicon.

  • What does your icon look like, in binary and/or hexadecimal?
    • My icon is a dash of lines. Almost like a pattern since I liked the vibe of it. IN binary form it was just black and white, but with hexadecimal it looked a lot better with color.
